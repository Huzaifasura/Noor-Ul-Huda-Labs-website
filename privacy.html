1. Children's Privacy & COPPA Compliance

Noor Ul Huda Kids is designed for children aged 5–15 under parental guidance. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent.

2. Information We Collect - Data Collection

Our app may collect limited information to improve the learning experience. This may include:

  • check_circle Student nickname (optional)
  • check_circle Avatar customization data
  • check_circle Learning progress and level progress
  • check_circle Stars, coins, achievements, and badges
  • check_circle Quiz statistics such as accuracy, correct answers, questions answered, etc.
  • check_circle Daily learning streaks and activity tracking
  • check_circle Salah tracking progress (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)
  • check_circle Anonymous usage analytics and app performance data

This information is used only to track learning progress, improve the educational experience, and personalize the app features.

📸 Images Policy:

If the app displays or loads images, they may be stored temporarily in the device's local cache to improve performance. These images are not stored in our database and are not uploaded to our servers.
